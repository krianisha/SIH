About AyurSwasthya

AyurSwasthya is an innovative project designed for the SMART INDIA HACKATHON 2024, blending the power of AI with ancient Ayurvedic wisdom. Our AI-driven system predicts diseases based on user symptoms and recommends personalized Ayurvedic remedies to restore balance and health.

With a sleek and user-friendly frontend built using ReactJS, robust backend with Node.js, and AI algorithms powered by Django/Flask combined with TensorFlow or PyTorch, AyurSwasthya merges cutting-edge technology with the timeless health practices of Ayurveda.
